      Mrs Dalloway

Mrs Dalloway is a novel by Virginia Woolf, published on 14 May 1925,[1] that details a day in the life of Clarissa Dalloway, a fictional upper-class woman in post-First World War England. It is one of Woolf's best-known novels.
